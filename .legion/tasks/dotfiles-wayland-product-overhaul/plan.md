# Dotfiles Wayland Product Overhaul

Mirrored task contract from the main workspace. Goal: refactor Linux workstation dotfiles to a Wayland-first Hyprland + UWSM + DMS/Quickshell product with Zen as primary browser, scoped mpv/Discord multimedia, reliable Bluetooth/Wi-Fi, hlissner-style Steam tuning, and Darwin-safe shared boundaries. Secure Boot, old X11/bspwm compatibility, broad multimedia expansion, and Darwin desktop feature work are out of scope.

Implementation in this worktree follows the approved RFC and review evidence mirrored under `docs/`.
